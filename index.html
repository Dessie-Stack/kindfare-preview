Support healthy function

Cellular Routine

A gentle daily rhythm built around your conditions — not a promise, a pattern worth repeating.
Metabolic and cardiovascular health
The habits in this routine are associated with better sleep timing, insulin sensitivity, muscle maintenance, cardiovascular fitness and overall wellbeing.
Consistency over intensity
Small actions repeated consistently are the goal. The routine avoids promises such as reversing ageing, treating fatigue or directly renewing mitochondria.
Benefits supported by the routine
Energy rhythm
Morning daylight supports circadian rhythm, sleep timing and daytime alertness, which can indirectly support metabolic health.
Muscle and metabolism
Movement and strength training help maintain muscle, improve insulin sensitivity, support mitochondrial content in muscle and reduce age-related muscle loss.
Recovery and repair
Sleep supports recovery, immune function, cognition and many cellular repair processes. Slow breathing can support stress regulation.
